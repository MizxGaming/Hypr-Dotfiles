# Waybar Features
- Click on the `Day - Time` module to switch between `Time` and `Calendar`
- Click on the Currently Playing module to play or pause currently playing media
- Click on the CPU usage module to switch between percentage and micro graph view
- Click on the Network module to switch displaying total bandwidth and separated upload and download speeds
- Click on the Bluetooth module to open up `blueberry` (needs to be installed)
- For the Sound module:
  + Left click to mute the Output/Input
  + Right click to open up `pavucontrol` and adjust sound options
- For the notifications module:
  + Left Click to open up notification panel
  + Right click to toggle DND
